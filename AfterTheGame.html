After the Game The game is over if one of two things happen:

1) All surviving humans make it to the extraction point with five signal flags
or
2) The Zombies have captured all of the humans.

When the game finishes, be sure to gather everyone together to celebrate the game, share stories and take photos.

Afterwards, remember to retrieve all of the game puzzles and the equipment at the Hospital and Armory.

Congratulations for completing the your Adventure Game!
