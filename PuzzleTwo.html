Puzzle 2: The Bin and the Birds At the location of Puzzle 2, players will find three things that will impact the game:

1) A Gun and Bullet from the Armory will be placed and can be picked up by any player.

2) A Hazardous Medical Waste Bin will be placed somewhere. If a player touches or picks up this, the Game Master can select that player to be examined by the Zombie Virus Detector App.

3) A Bird Poster left behind by the Scientist contains the important clue to the location of Puzzle 3. Location Enter the location of Puzzle 2: Puzzle 2 Location
Puzzle The Bird Poster contains illustrations of over 25 common North American birds. The Scientist discovered that one of the birds was carrying the Zombie Virus and during his studies, he cut out the illustration of that bird to hang on his wall at the lab. The missing bird in the poster will lead players to the location of Puzzle 3 when players read the note left about that bird in the Bird Journal. You'll hang the Bird Poster somewhere at the Puzzle 2 Location.
