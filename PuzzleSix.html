Puzzle 6: Panic Button You've suddenly discovered that the Nurse has become a Zombie Leader and has infected all players sent to the Hospital. You've decided that it is imperative to gather the remaining survivors and evacuate the area.

To warn any survivoring humans and to tell them about your plan, you've recorded a message and placed a Panic Button at the Puzzle 6 location. Location Enter the location of Puzzle 6:
(Pro Tip: Puzzle 6 will lead players back to the Armory) Puzzle 6 Location
Puzzle Humans must locate the Panic Button, press it and listen to your recorded message. You will have up to 20 seconds for your message. A typical message will indicate the situation and where surviving humans should meet. For example: “If you're hearing this, I have located the body of the Scientist and that's the good news. The bad news is that the Nurse has turned into a Zombie Leader and infected others. I need all surviving humans to meet me at the Armory as soon as possible while avoiding any roaming Zombies. Good luck!”


Record Your Message:

You'll use the Talking Button to record your message and use for Puzzle 6.

1. Insert (2) AAA Batteries into the Talking Button.
2. Switch the underside button to "REC".
3. Press and hold the red button until you hear a single beep.
4. Continue to hold the button
5. Say your message. Remember - you're really stressed out about the Zombies so make sure there is urgency and angst in your voice!
6. Release the button and you will hear two beeps.
7. Switch the underside button to "PLAY"
8. Press the red button to hear your important message.
9. Optional: if you wish to re-record your message, repeats the steps 2-7 above.


Place the Panic Buton:

On Game Day, you'll place the Panic Button somewhere at the Puzzle 6 Location.
