Activity 3: Prepare for War The surviving humans have returned to the Armory to prepare for their escape and hopeful evacuation from the area.

While the humans prepare in the Armory, all of the players who have turned into Zombies have returned to the Hospital to put on their Zombie Makeup and Zombie Plaid Shirt. After they are dressed and gathered, the Nurse tells the Zombies where the Puzzle 7 area is located and their new goal to convert all of the humans to Zombies. If the Zombies are successful in capturing every surviving huan, then the Zombie Horde wins the game. Location Enter the location of the Armory:
(Pro Tip: the Armory should be close to the Kickoff and the Hospital) Activity 3 Location
Activity The remaining humans return to the Armory to gather weapons and create their strategy for securing the five Signal Flags and reaching the Extraction Point. You will update the group on their situation and what they need to escape ZombieWorld

"It's clear that we now have no choice but to leave this place. Everyone should grab at least one gun and some ammunition. The local Army chapter has a helicopter that can pick up all of us and fly to safety but we have to have Signal Flags and be at the Extaction Point. The Signal Flags are located nearby (explain). Take some time to come up with your strategy to work together and when I think the coast is clear, I'll return and we'll begin making our way to the Signal Flags."

Over at the Hospital, the Nurse will be instructing the Zombies to:

"The humans will be attempting to escape by reaching an Extraction Point that is marked with a Signal Flare light. Before they get to the Extraction Point, they must gather five Signal Flags that can be used to flag down an Army Helicopter. The Extraction Point and Signal Flags are located nearby (explain). As the Zombie Horde, you will now position yourselves out around the Signal Flags and Extraction Point with the goal of turning each surviving human into a Zombie. Should each surviving human become Zombie, then the Zombie Horde will be declared the victors of the game. If one or more surviving humans reach the Extraction Point with all five Signal Flags, then they will be declared the victors. Remember that if you are struck with a Nerf Bullet, you will be stunned and frozen for 5 seconds. After you count 5 Mississippi, you can resume your pursuit of any human."

After all of the Zombies have been dressed and made their way to the Puzzle 7 area, you will able to return to the Armory and allow the humans to begin their escape.


Hint:
1. Stay together and work together repel the Zombie horde.
2. Bullets are valuable... try to conserve them.
3. A bullet hit will stun a Zombie for five seconds... it won't kill them but it will give you time to escape
