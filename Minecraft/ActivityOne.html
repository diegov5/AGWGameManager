Activity 1: Crafting Table Scavenger Hunt A Crafting Table lets use build things needed to solve the adventure. The Woods Biome contains 4 Wood Pieces that can be used to make the Crafting Table. When all 4 pieces are found, the group should assemble the table. Once the table is made a volunteer from the group must agree to carry it during the adventure.

Suggested Script for the Game Host:
Well, what does everyone think?
Haha I’m stumped, get it, cuz we’re in the forest?

*if none of the kids understand what is going on*
It says here you can find scattered pieces to build a craftsmen table, that’s pretty important, I bet if you look around this area we’ll discover the wood pieces needed to build it! Go on get, I’m still exhausted by my defeat so I’ll wait right here, and once you gather all four pieces, we’ll brainstorm what do do next!

*kids find all four pieces and gather back with the host*
Wow that was fast! I remember my days as a younger, more novice crafter, ah those were the good days… Well anyways, how do you think we’ll build this?

*kids and Host construct the craftsmen table*
Nice! We did it… who wants to be caretaker of this here, special, craftsmen table, it’s an important job...who here thinks that can take the responsibility?

*kid volunteers
