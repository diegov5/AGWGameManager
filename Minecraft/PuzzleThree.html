Puzzle The Bird Watching Scope points to the location of Puzzle 4. The Scope has 4x optical magnification and can show a building or object from 100's of yards away.

Pick a location for Puzzle 4 that is a fair distance away from Puzzle 3 that the Scope can easily point at and where thereâ€™s a good solution to mount the Scope.

You can use the Bungie Cord to lash the Scope to an existing pole, sign or suitable object. You'll want the Scope to be very secure so that it cannot be moved easily.

Optionally, you can build your own mounting solution and install it at the Puzzle 3 location.


Mount the Scope:

Determine how and where you'll want to mount the Scope at the location of Puzzle 3. On Game Day, you'll install the Scope and make sure it's properly pointing to the location of Puzzle 4.



Hint
1. Don't put your eye right up against a Scope. Instead, look through the scope from 1-2" away.

2. The bird's are long gone but the Scope probably still points to something of interest!
