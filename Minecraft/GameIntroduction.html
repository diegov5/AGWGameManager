Game Introduction For the Game Introduction, the Game Host will outline the goal of the Adventure Game, and rally up the heros to the cause!

Suggested Script for the Game Host:

Yes yes yes! Thank you all- it means so much to get some help!, and heck, what do you know, maybe I’ll share some of my life treasures if we are to succeed and defeat that malicious dragon.
Problem is - We have a lot of work to do, and I mean a lot….
To get to the dragon, we’ll have to track down a portal that will transport us to the other realm, the End.
However, to unlock the portal we need to find twelve ender eyes. These eyes are scattered all across this realm.
This will be a tough journey, but with our courage and might, we just might be able to pull this off.

At this point, the Game Host will also provide one map to each participant of the game, and explain the different biomes as follows:

Oh! I almost forgot, I’ve saved a bunch of extra maps over my lifetime, here, why don’t you all take one so you can take a good look of all the different biomes we have in this realm!
*Points out each biome to the kids*
Here we have the desert biome, this is the swamp biome, oh, and here’s the woods… It’s crucial we gather these eyes so we can travel to the End, so let’s get a move on everybody!!!
*host begins to start running, but then stops, remembers something else*
