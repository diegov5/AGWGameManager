Puzzle 7: Signal Flags The Signal Flags required to call the Army Helicopter have been scattered in an area nearby. The surviving humans must secure all five Signal Flags before reaching the Extraction Point.

You will install the five Signal Flags in the Puzzle 7 Area. You can decide to lay the flags on the ground, stand them in the ground or attach them to objects. Location Enter the location of Puzzle 7:
(Pro Tip: the area for Puzzle 7 should be located between the Armory and the Extraction Point)
(Pro Tip: the Puzzle 7 area should be large enough to let you plant five Signal Flags in different locations)
Puzzle 7 Location
Puzzle You will place each Signal Flag in a separate location in a larger area. You can control the difficultly by defining the distance and visibility of the Signal Flags. For example, it will be easier if the flags are in sight of each other and placed close to each other. To make things more challenging, you can lengthen the distance between flags.

There will be a lot of excitement in this puzzle as humans run to pick up the flags while trying to avoid Zombies. Make sure the area for the flags is appropriate and safe for running and tag.

Once the humans have secured all five Signal Flags, they must get to the Extraction Point. Any combination of humans and Signal Flags will work. For example, one human could be carrying all five Signal Flags and the other humans could reach the Extraction Point without any Signal Flags. Once all Signal Flags have been delivered to the Extraction Point, any humans that arrive at the Extraction Point will be saved and will be victors in the game.



Hint

1. Communicate so that your fellow humans know how many Signal Flags have been picked up.
2. If you can't find a Signal Flag, keep moving and keep looking in the same area.
