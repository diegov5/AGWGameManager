Set Up The Safe:

You'll need to set the combination of the safe to use the combination you entered as a clue in Puzzle 4.

1. Set the combination to 0-0-0 and turn the knob clockwise to open.
2. On the inside of the safe cover, move the lever from A to B.
3. Set the combination to 1-2-3 (This is the combination you entered in the Puzzle 4 clue)
4. With the cover still open, turn the knob counter-clockwise to the lock position.
5. On the inside of the safe cover, move the lever from B to A.

Your new combination is now set and ready to hold the Experimental Vaccine Syringe.

1. Open the safe cover.
2. Place one Experimental Vaccine Syringe in the safe.
3. Option: Place anything else you wish in the safe.
4. Close the safe cover.
5. Mix up the combination on the outside cover.

Place the Small Safe:

On Game Day, you’ll place the Small Safe somewhere at the Puzzle 5 Location. You can optionally use the Security Cable to secure the case to a fixed object.

Set Up The Laboratory Note Card Puzzle:

Players can use the Laboratory Note Card to write down the Subject Names of the Microscope Slides. The Scientist has carefully listed the order of the subjects he was studying. After the corresponding Subject Name is written for each Slide Number, the clue begins to emerge. The first letter of each Subject Name spells the clue leading to Puzzle 6.

Your clue can be between 1 and 25 characters long to form a word or a short phrase. Punctuation like apostrophes, hyphens and periods are ignored.

Your clue can be simple and obvious like “The Shed” or it can be more challenging as a riddle “Where the yard tools live”.
